The Best Animes According to Akash

My Top 3 Animes of all-time.

Attack on Titan

A world where humanity has been driven to the brink of extinction and lives within three enormous concentric walls to protect themselves from giant, man-eating humanoid creatures known as Titans. The series begins with a century of peace, during which the main characters—Eren Yeager, his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their best friend Armin Arlert—live a seemingly normal life in the outermost wall, Wall Maria. This fragile peace is violently shattered by the sudden appearance of the Colossal Titan, a gargantuan creature that breaches the wall, allowing other Titans to swarm inside. In the chaos that follows, Eren witnesses a Titan devour his mother, sparking a deep-seated hatred and a burning desire for revenge. This traumatic event sets him on a path to join the Scout Regiment, an elite military branch that ventures beyond the walls, with the sole purpose of exterminating every last Titan.

Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, a boisterous, orphaned, and lonely young boy living in the Hidden Leaf Village. Twelve years before the story begins, a powerful nine-tailed fox demon attacked the village, and the Fourth Hokage, the village's leader, sacrificed his life to seal the demon inside a newborn Naruto. As a result, Naruto is ostracized and feared by the villagers, who see him not as a hero but as the monster itself. Desperate for attention and recognition, Naruto often acts out by pulling pranks and causing mischief, but his true dream is to become the next Hokage and finally earn the respect and admiration of everyone in his village.

Dragon Ball Z

Goku, five years after the events of "Dragon Ball," now a grown man and the father of a young boy named Gohan. The peaceful life he has built is shattered by the arrival of a powerful, long-lost brother named Raditz. Raditz reveals to Goku that he is not from Earth but is a Saiyan, a member of a nearly extinct warrior race that sent him to destroy the planet. After kidnapping Gohan to coerce Goku into joining him, Raditz's immense power forces Goku to form an unlikely alliance with his former rival, Piccolo. The two must work together in a desperate attempt to defeat this formidable new foe and save the world.
